A little about me

Creative spirit, curious heart

I'm Asma — a designer who finds inspiration in the world around her. Whether it's the geometry of a forest canopy, the colour palette of a spice market, or the handwriting on a menu in a tucked-away café, beauty is everywhere if you know how to look.

My creative work is shaped by travel and connection. Every new place I visit teaches me something new about colour, form, and the human experience — and I carry those lessons back into everything I make.

Food, for me, is never just food. It's a conversation with a culture, a memory in the making, and one of the most honest forms of design there is.
